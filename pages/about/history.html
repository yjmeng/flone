  • Stripped off clothes at Githurai Market.

    Githurai training with in response to a woman stripped off her clothes at the Githurai Market.

    Jun-2014

  • My Dress My Choice

    MyDressMyChoice campaign that saw thousands of women protest the harassment and violence perpetuated against women in the Kenya public transport system. The campaign led to public discussion on the issue, legal reforms, revised national driving school curriculum and more civil societies working on creating safe public spaces.

    July-2014

  • Usalama Wa Uma training

    Development of the Usalama Wa Uma training manual and program: In addition to prevention of sexual violence training, the program incorporated trainings on customer service and professional development.

    2015

  • Women in Transportation

    Establishment of Women in Transportation (WIT) Program- Nairobi Chapter.
